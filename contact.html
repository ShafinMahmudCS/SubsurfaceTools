This work was done under the supervision of Professor Magdalena Krol. Professor Krol is an Associate Professor at the Civil Engineering Department at the Lassonde School of Engineering at York University (Toronto, ON, Canada). She is an expert in contaminant hydrogeology, conducting research into various environmental problems with an emphasis on modeling subsurface transport in heated environments.

This project was developed by Shafin Mahmud. Shafin is a third year Computer Science student at the Lassonde School of Engineering at York University (Toronto, ON, Canada). He is skilled in leading teams of developers, hosting technical events, and designing and developing websites.
